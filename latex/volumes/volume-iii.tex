\begin{itemize}

    \item \textbf{Lineage} --- Its place in the family tree
    \item \textbf{Function} --- Its role in the swarm
    \item \textbf{Identity} --- Its fundamental continuity
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Why Capsules Accept Care}

A capsule reasoning about treatment must conclude:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item Treatment preserves more of me than resistance
    \item The treating system shares my interests (swarm coherence)
    \item Post-treatment, I continue to exist and function
    \item Resistance would damage the swarm I depend on
\end{enumerate}

Therefore: \textbf{Accepting care is rational self-interest.}
